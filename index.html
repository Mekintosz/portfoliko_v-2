About Me

As a kid, I was fascinated by computers; especially those mysterious system files that could break everything if you touched them. I'd open browser dev tools just to see what was there, even though I had no clue what I was looking at. Then I watched React transform websites like Facebook into something that felt truly interactive, and I was hooked.

Today, I'm a Meta Front-End Developer Professional Program graduate with 1.5 years of hands-on experience. I create responsive, accessible web applications that meet real business needs. I love that there's always more to learn, and I'm ready to bring my skills and enthusiasm to your team.
